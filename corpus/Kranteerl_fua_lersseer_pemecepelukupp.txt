krante edixa



Tletima:


Edixa pemecepelukupp (xut PMS z’es pi'ene'd menisesse'd sarmavirle) vatin fal feti'aven lkurftlesse'd far zelx liaxi voles malefikinavo penul lanerme icco'lt faust linaest adit vefisait, vers lanerme zu lkurf lanerme lkurftlessess i lerj iftorn. Edixa iftorn io lipalain fankas z'ilyr penul lanerme icco i lkurftlessen parcdirxel cix maubesines foiiumeso lineparine. Fai la lex, fankasa'ct l'ilyr iccoss verlet kantio'c lineparine. El la lex fenteno cix mol fasmaler'd fagrigeciorisustu's fal phil.1998. Edixa la lex ircalart lot is etixonj cun lineparine vatin cixj sarmavirle nisse'dy fal penul lanerme icco'd avir zu es lavirleni'arjtol. La dyst adit hartlirfa, juviedicco io fallerrgen lkurftlesse'd evirlerfalveltavol faij lex kjilf lineparine fantaspoluri'dy es ladir PMS'dy. PMCF'd yst lkurfal at io celternon lex lus foblerkolank cix don cun la lex veles lidysto fai nastan pa lkurftlessestan'it fagrigecio cix lkurfer es vers. Mal, la lexe'd waxfalvit es sxe fai juviedicco adit elme, lkurfer'd fagrigecio. 
Nestil io gliftqun'ovili'a lanerme ayplerd stiesonj PMS'i "kada linepaine" (harda lineparine) adit "culup lukup" (stylup la lkurf)'c pelx liaxu no io lkurftlessestan veles stieso "pemecepe lukup" (PMCF'd lkurftless)'c mal yst lkurfal at io suite la lex. Pa, niv mol kanteluesco's zu veles laoziao hark zu vi'artones serafarsastan ad na's fai nestile'd fanknen moliupi'avostan mag nat ixvarlafirlex eso PMS feut lasj lkurferl lanermeonj lineparine'it. Yst lkurfal aprart le anka lot vatino ad cierjustelo iccoer'st fon la cierjustel lerj lkurftlessestan'd fryrcerlex fai panqa'd foiiumuste cix lojo kanteluesco.


Yst lkurfal fon pi’ene’d menisesse’d chafi’ofese’d fankas krante.
