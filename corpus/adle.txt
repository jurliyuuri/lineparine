Valkarsa'd puderrgil io lartass elmerlst jan

D'la neydoss, la sdeumen'c katuroj zuj zelk,

jungkar'd la liestumil'c nyi xale is hattva

Lecu miss es tesarja'i fua lartass'd cerke!

Shrlo taty la xoleu flaniety'l

Afnar lektonj delmerdz ly

faxamorlu la fhark'i shrlo kun

alcames da lartass'd elmeuss!

La harda festena es forme mylon'dy

La lex es aleron lex karxeu la irld

Bekarlen la arte'd la terxe's ny'i l' l' flirant

La nissen elmergeu'd la elmo alt jan
