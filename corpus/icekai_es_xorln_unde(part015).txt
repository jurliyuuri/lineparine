14te larfa Elmelesti

Liaxi Dethik plasi mels elmel. Letixer loler morsacecanfy ad fentemorsanfy lus morsen elmel l'es zant mal letixer loler kheltececanfy ad fentekheltanfy lus khelt. La lex es vynut fal farfel ly. Pa la lex m'es niv stilkernerfen, pit'i lex lus elmel at mol ly. Edixa lesback io Harlina ad dqa'd lartass text morsen elmal mal mi ad Turlina lap text khelten elmel. Edixu Dethik lus khelt fal elmo pelx cene melo leus elm fal cirla melx, jol si is kynte fua pit ly. Mal, no ler miss kavani'ajl fafcives. Edixa fontles veles kantio Dethike'st paltdzarl'it zelx deluses fua elmo leusj khelt. Cene miss gylerylon lus khelt xale farfel mal,...... “Liaoll coss lus khelt fal elx elmil niv at?” Edixu piter jel la ny farte'c mels la lex. La lexen es cun, niryjen lartass farfelon lus khelt fal laoziavil dexafel adit lusil ietost ly. Edixa mi niv xel eso e'i Dethike'st cun mi celdin niv si mels jujoj lyme. Deliu mi teles e'i fal fasta. Mal, itanisamalne g'es, plaxeson veles plasio lerj yst panqa. Cene khelt volied la fontals faller als voleso pelx, kheltesnej'i dexamols fai xerfesnej voleso'dy. Mal, lex volied volesov'i tisodrenejto es jurlet fua luso khelt. Mag, tisodenerle'c vivynen lkurferl l'es lirtuel ciant mol mal, e celdin luso khelt ly. “Klirma io <dexafelesosti> adit <ietost lidystosti> es dulesen fua starsk ja lu. Edixa vel xici'st lex lus <kafi'aven la xejisti> at es panqa'd ciant. Marta, khelt fua elmo's fafciv'i deluses fal farfel. Liaxa khelt'i lenivifarlo niv pa, panqate ankalefeneso io ises. Vel cixi letix kirxniarx ja lu.” Jol pesta io mol lanxervavol mag jurkenelen anfi'e fedi. Tan, jol liaxa ankaalon firlex mels khelt. Mal lecu fafcives fal no. “Hame mole miss fafcives?” “Mi kanti cossa'c fhasfasnej'd khelt mag ankalefeneson lus la lex plax.” Edixa Dethik kanti missa'c fhasfa'd khelt ad ciantass fua la lex mal, deson tydiest zanterssa'l.
