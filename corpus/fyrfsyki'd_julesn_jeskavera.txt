fyrfsyki'd jeskaverasti


alsasti, Edixa co lenivifarlo senosto "jeskavera"?
metista, wioll co set senost la lex fal selxiunk.
la lex es tisodel fon irfel jeska zu es xola'd carxa.
wioll mi lkurf mels jeskavera fua menass zu qune niv jeskavera.


1te krantiestaium : yuesleone'd xolasti


irfel jeska es yuesleoneen xola'd klantez kynte.
edixu yuesleone io dznojuli'o'd dolisn lartass afla desnux larta fal penul liestu. edixa irfel jeska cierjustel yuesleone'd lartassa'd nyrtaten vletwal irfel jumili'a zu es irfel jeska'd viojunsarkh i ad aflavo'it lex veles virben lartassa'tj. 
irfel jeska's snenstj yletta cix moliupi'a filx normta fai ja yst lkurfo.
pa, jol dolisn lartassastan is afuzarfrirga. edixa liestustan io dolisn lartass'it tysnenerss'i lex ydicel dolisn lartass alf elx edixu elmerss irfel jeska'tj.
________________


pa, irfel jeska ysev elmerl'i. irfel jeska cierjustel yuesleone'd medarne'd lertasal niv alfersse'tj.
irfel jeska ad chaku fon yuesleone'd medarne'd lertasal tysnen yuesleone'd dolisn lartass mal tilien klantez yuesleone'd xol mal laozia yuesleone'd fankas.


2te krantiestaium : farl'liurdi'a'd chirfar


irfel jeska es fontlesa'd klantez kyluseser fon yuesleone'd medarne'd lertasal.
irfel jeska's jeskavera'd yletta gylery cix lkurf farl'liurdi'a'd chirfar fal laoziavil la lex. Jol la lex es koni'endeili'a fua als cun la lex veles kranteo leusj snietij lkurfel. mag, selene mi plasi mels farl'liurdi'a'd chirfar fua als.
________________


1. fqa io miss tvasnk birleeso navax fua iccer adit iccer fua icco, icco fua iccer, iccer fua navax.


la lex kantet ny la lex. fallerrgen miss es chaku fon klantez vixiticco zu irfel jeska laozia. Mal, deliu miss ecean snoj mels vixiticcostan mal ylettartisod la lex.


2. miss parle delusolacergen la sietiv fua iccer mal les seldi'aveno cix veles tvasnko la vi'art ad la lajnef fal kykol mal es phitali'eneerl ad kanstakt'i fua fallerrgen hartlirfa adit anfejatz, navax. la lex es lertasal fergen'artzerl panqatej.


la lex kantet ny la lex. deliu fankasa'd chakuss malfarno niv mels arte iulo ad lidesneso. deliu als sietivel veles moliupi'a yletta mors io.


3. icco parle tvasnko'd flenti'a fal laprysten nefxituavol iccer'd nejorto ad sietivo ad icco.


        la lex kantet ny la lex. cene miss tvasnk als tonir.
pa, cene niv miss corln tvasnk tonir zelx jol es dolisn iulo'i el irfel jeska'd lertasal ad misse'd vixiticco.
________________


4. lertasal fergen'artzerl cix text lkurftlesse'd eustiravera faus miss. Deliu d'icco, fallergen juviedicco parle lkurftless zu veles lkurfo fal fallerrgen juviedicco mal la lex io lex sietiv hartlirfa letix i. 


misse'd vixiticco'd lartass lus sxe lkurftless filx lineparine. edixa dolisn lartastan zu veles stieso "xelken" i arson celes lkurfo penul lineparine pelx irfel jeska'd lertasal es niv la lex'i mal yletta cix moliupi'a als lkurftless.


5. miss afnar niv alese'd nermetixaler adit akademice lineparine (ADLP), lipalain nermetixaler, ete'd als ecerharturl anfhiu ad elme. pa, fe lertasal ad icco es fhanka febikaxto'i el la lexe'd navaxastan ad larfastan.


la lex kantet ny la lex. klantez lartass es klantez pa icco nefisnavon moliupi'a niv la lex. misse'd vixiticco io als es vi'art.
6. iccer letix als daliu nirfe ad felecu fal sopit ad ditierrgeraven lartassa'd ny slyrs.


miss l'es iccer alsj letix daliu nirfe ad felecu fal vixiticco'st snojerl ad misse'd fusaf zelx jol zel ple na.
________________


7. miss phitali'ene xolodir fon ditzitis chaxorte mal fenten fentexolera zu faoves flenti'aven arterletera adit anharteranasch nasterrgera, kanternera, nyviepera, macciccovera, et. 


la lex kantet ny la lex. yletta cix moliupi'a xola'd tisodel filx lystetodir fon penul tisoderl.


8. miss afnar maceluatrelmer lerj yuesleone mal laozia celtern xolanasch sesnuden elminal.


la lex kantet ny la lex. lecu miss laozia ankaen elminal failx verletol la lex e'ct fal starsk filx deroko elmerss mels delus. irfel jeska tydixes mels normta. pa, jol elmerss misse'ct elx mol. elminala'd delus es mieste fua sesnudo xola'd tisodel lerj elmerss xale la lex.


9. als acirl adit kanti'a, kantio'it veleso elx deliu es flentia lut iulo.


la lex kantet ny la lex. misse'st qanteo es flenti'a lut iulo. zu, cene miss laozia als la sniror adit aklurpti'a, acirl.
pa, cene niv miss corln larkas iulo zelx jol es dolisn iulo'i el irfel jeska'd lertasal ad misse'd vixiticco.

fal lususil


alsasti, edixa coss firlex irfel jeska tisodel?
edixa kranteerlestan io mi lkurf farl'liurdi'a'd chirfar zu es jeskavera'd gylery i ad yuesleone'd xol zu festel jeskavera e'ct i fua diunangvirlo.
menass zelx selene qune le loler iulo mels jeskavera ad irfel jeska failx akrantio kranteerlestan i lecu lersse jeskavera fai akrantio "jeskavera'd krantzeler fua als".
Mi alpileon sinh iso coss faut snykyon virlierss jeskavera.
